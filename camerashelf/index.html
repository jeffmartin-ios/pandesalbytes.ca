Frequently Asked Questions

Where is my data stored?

Your gear list is personal. Data is stored locally on your device and synced to your other devices via your private iCloud account.

Does it support multiple currencies?

Yes. You can input purchase prices in different currencies, and the app will help you normalize the total value of your collection.

Can I organize non-camera gear?

Absolutely. Camera Shelf is perfect for tracking drones, lighting equipment, audio gear, and computer accessories alongside your cameras.
