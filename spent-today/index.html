Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data private?

Yes. All your data is stored securely on your device and in your private iCloud account. We never see or have access to your financial information.

Do I need a subscription?

No. We believe in owning your software. Spent Today offers powerful core features for free, and a single one-time purchase for premium access. No subscriptions, ever.

Can I export my data?

Yes, you can export your transaction history as a CSV file from the settings menu at any time.
