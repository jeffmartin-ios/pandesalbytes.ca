Simple Software, Honestly Crafted.

Based in Toronto, Canada, Pandesal Bytes is an independent studio with a simple philosophy: we build minimal, focused apps that we genuinely use ourselves. In a world of digital clutter, we believe in creating tools that are not just functional, but also a pleasure to use.

**What is a 'Pandesal'?** It's a slightly sweet, beloved breakfast roll from the Philippines. The name reflects our aim: to create simple, comforting, and essential digital "bites" (bytes) that are a delightful start to your day. We recommend you try one!

Our approach is straightforward. We identify a personal need, craft a beautiful solution, and polish it until it feels just right.

Our Commitment

Privacy First

We build our apps to be private by design. We don't collect or sell your personal data. Ever.

No Distractions

You won't find ads, trackers, or unnecessary notifications in our software.

Built to Last

We focus on quality and long-term support, ensuring our apps remain reliable and useful for years to come.

Own Your Apps. Ditch the Subscriptions

All of our apps are free to use with powerful core features. If you want to level up, premium access is just a one-time purchase away. We're not fans of everything needing subscriptions, so we'll never ask you for one.

Thank you for supporting independent development. If you have any questions or feedback, we'd love to hear from you.
