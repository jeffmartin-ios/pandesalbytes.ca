Frequently Asked Questions

Does it import from websites?

Yes! Recipe Mini can extract ingredients and instructions from most popular cooking websites automatically.

Can I scale servings?

Absolutely. You can adjust the serving size, and the app will automatically recalculate the ingredient quantities for you.

Is there a cooking mode?

Yes, Step-by-Step mode keeps your screen on and lets you focus on one instruction at a time, so you never lose your place.
