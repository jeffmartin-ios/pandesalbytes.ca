Frequently Asked Questions

Which cameras are supported?

We currently support custom recipe storage for most modern Fujifilm X-Series cameras and Ricoh GR cameras.

Is my data private?

Yes. Your recipes are stored locally on your device and synced securely via your private iCloud. We do not have access to your library.

Can I import recipes?

Yes, you can import recipes from other users via QR code, making it easy to try out new looks from the community.
