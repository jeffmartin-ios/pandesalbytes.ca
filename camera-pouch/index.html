Frequently Asked Questions

Is this for professionals or hobbyists?

Both! Camera Pouch is designed to be simple enough for a hobbyist planning a weekend shoot, but powerful enough for a professional managing clients and complex projects.

Is my data private?

Yes. All your project data, client lists, and locations are stored privately on your device and synced securely via your personal iCloud account. We never see your data.

Does the sun tracker work offline?

Yes, the core sun position calculations work entirely offline, making it reliable even when you're shooting in remote locations without cell service.
